Instructions

Notakto is played on a finite number of empty three-by-three boards. Each player takes turns placing an X on the board(s) in a vacant space. If a board has a three-in-a-row, the board is dead and it cannot be played on any more. When one player makes a three-in-a-row and there are no more boards to play on, that player loses.
